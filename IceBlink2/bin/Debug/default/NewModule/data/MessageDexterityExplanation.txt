<b>Dexterity (DEX):</b> How agile, nimble and precise a player character is. This influences order of initiative in combat, ranged to hit rolls, armor class (especially when wearing light armor), Reflex saving rolls and chances for hiding in shadows during combat. It is also relevant for picking physical locks, disarming physical traps and sneaking on the main map.
